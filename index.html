solid waste comprises of all the wastes arising from human and animal activities that are typically solid and that are discarded as useless or unwanted.

Problem: India produces 277 million tonnes of municipal solid waste every year, which is likely to touch 387.8 million tones in 2030 AND 543.3 million tonnes by 2050 due to "rapid urbanisation,population growth and economic development 

Solution : Dividing waste into dry and wet. (BIODEGRADBLE AND NON-BIODEGRADABLE). Dry waste includes wood and related products, metals and glass. In addition, always remember to Reduce, Reuse and Recycle
